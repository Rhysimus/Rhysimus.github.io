Aria Disconnect

Aria Disconnect is a 16-bit action-adventure. You are the protagonist Heri and you are going to follow the journey of his life crisis. The year is 2082, you are hooked to a machine, and there is only one way out, to disconnect.

Kimmo Savilampi is the wonder behind all of this. I'm helping create systems like this ring menu, but more notably large scale projects like a water physics system and a live midi player (more on this at a later date - look out on YouTube!).

The Impact of Computers in Medical Conditions

This is a piece of work I created for my EPQ, resulting in an A*.

It covers how computers and technology has been used within prominent conditions - from general applications of hospital information systems to the care of critically ill patients.

Links are then made between the conditions and these uses, with suggestions and current uses specifically towards them.

The future of technology in medicine is also explored here, to bring it all together to conclude the impact of computers in medical conditions.
